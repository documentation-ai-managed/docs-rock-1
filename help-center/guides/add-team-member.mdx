## Steps to Add a Team Member

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to your workspace settings">
    Click the workspace name and select "Settings".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select 'Team'">
    Go to the "Team" tab in the settings menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite your teammate">
    Click "Invite member", enter their email address, and choose their role.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send invitation">
    Click "Send Invite". They will receive an email to join.
  </Step>
</Steps>
