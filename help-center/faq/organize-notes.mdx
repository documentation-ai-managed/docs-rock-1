## Organizing Notes

- Create workspaces for different projects.
- Use topics to categorize notes.
- Pin important notes to the top of a workspace.
- Use tags for easy searching.
- Archive old notes to reduce clutter.

To move a note:
1. Open the note.
2. Click on the "More" menu (three dots).
3. Select "Move to Workspace" and choose the destination.

<Callout kind="tip">Tags help you find notes quickly.</Callout>
