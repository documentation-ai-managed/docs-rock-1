## Overview

Rock serves as your centralized documentation hub, enabling you to organize, create, and manage all project documentation in one intuitive space. You streamline workflows by nesting pages, adding rich content, and collaborating seamlessly with your team. You gain efficiency by centralizing scattered docs into a single source of truth. Teams reduce onboarding time, minimize errors from outdated info, and foster innovation through easy knowledge sharing. Projects stay on track with clear progress tracking and integrated workflows.

## Getting Started

Follow these steps to set up your first Rock workspace and create a page.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">

    Visit `https://app.rock.co.uk` and create your account with email or SSO.

  </Step>

  <Step title="Create Workspace" icon="folder-plus">

    Name your workspace (e.g., "Project Alpha") and invite team members via email.

  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Your First Page" icon="file-plus">

    Click "New Page" and start editing. Here's a quick example:

    <CodeGroup tabs="Markdown,MDX">
    ````markdown
    # My First Page

    Welcome to Rock!

    - List item 1
    - List item 2
    ````

    ````mdx
    ## Structured Content

    Use components like `<Callout>` for highlights.

    ```javascript
    console.log("Hello, Rock!");
    ```
    ````
    </CodeGroup>

  </Step>
</Steps>
